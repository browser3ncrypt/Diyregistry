Overview

yt-dlp is a command-line program to download videos and audio from YouTube and thousands of other websites. It is actively maintained with frequent compatibility updates.

Official repository: https://github.com/yt-dlp/yt-dlp

Installation & Update

  • Standalone binary: Download from GitHub Releases
  • Via pip: python3 -m pip install -U "yt-dlp[default]"
  • Update: yt-dlp -U (or --update-to nightly for latest fixes)

Command Categories

General syntax: yt-dlp [OPTIONS] "URL"

**Summary:** Basic video + audio downloads 
yt-dlp "https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID"
**Summary:** Video-only or audio-only extraction 
yt-dlp -f bestvideo "URL"
yt-dlp -f bestaudio "URL"
yt-dlp -x --audio-format mp3 "URL"
**Summary:** Listing and selecting specific formats 
yt-dlp -F "URL"
yt-dlp -f 137+140 "URL"
**Summary:** Playlists & range selection 
yt-dlp "PLAYLIST_URL"
yt-dlp --playlist-items 1,5,10-15 "URL"
**Summary:** Subtitles, chapters & metadata 
yt-dlp --write-auto-sub --sub-lang en --convert-subs srt "URL"
yt-dlp --write-thumbnail --embed-thumbnail "URL"
yt-dlp --embed-chapters --embed-metadata "URL"
**Summary:** SponsorBlock integration 
yt-dlp --sponsorblock-remove all "URL"
**Summary:** Download archive (avoid re-downloads) 
yt-dlp --download-archive archive.txt "URL"
**Summary:** Rate limiting & retries 
yt-dlp --limit-rate 2M "URL"
yt-dlp --retries 10 "URL"
**Summary:** Cookies & authentication 
yt-dlp --cookies-from-browser chrome "URL"
**Summary:** Batch downloading 
yt-dlp -a urls.txt
**Summary:** Custom output templates 
yt-dlp -o "%(upload_date)s - %(title)s [%(id)s].%(ext)s" "URL"

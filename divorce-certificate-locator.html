Information compiled from official state sources and CDC guidance as of early 2026. Divorce decrees are court records and are usually obtained from the clerk of the court (district/circuit/superior) in the county where the divorce was granted. Divorce certificates (short-form abstracts) are issued by state vital records offices in only some states and for more recent years. Fees and processing times are approximate and subject to change. Eligibility is often restricted (parties to the divorce, attorneys, immediate family, etc.). Always verify directly on the official website. Many states partner with VitalChek for expedited certificate orders where applicable.

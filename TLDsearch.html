Note on Pricing: Cloudflare Registrar offers domains at-cost (wholesale registry price + ICANN fee where applicable, with no markup). Prices are the same for registration, renewal, and transfer (except for a few TLDs with premium pricing). Cloudflare does not publish a public pricing table due to registry agreements. Actual prices can vary slightly over time and are shown during checkout in the Cloudflare dashboard. For the most accurate and current prices, search for a domain at domains.cloudflare.com or check third-party sites like cfdomainpricing.com or tld-list.com.
